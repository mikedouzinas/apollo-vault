# Welcome to Apollo Vault

Your vault, but it thinks.

## Getting Started

1. **Run the setup wizard**: `/init-bootstrap`
   - This walks you through configuring everything
   - It learns about you and tailors the vault to your workflow

2. **Import your history** (optional):
   - Drop files into this Inbox folder before or during setup
   - Chat exports from ChatGPT, Gemini, or any LLM
   - Existing notes, project docs, anything about you or your work
   - Apollo will process and organize them

3. **Try these commands**:
   - `/thinking-partner` - Explore ideas through questions
   - `/council` - Get multiple perspectives on a hard problem
   - `/daily-review` - End of day reflection
   - `/weekly-synthesis` - Weekly pattern recognition

## How Apollo Is Different

- **Your CLAUDE.md is alive**: It grows and updates as you use the vault
- **Proactive context**: Claude loads relevant files before you ask
- **Council**: Think through problems from multiple genuine perspectives
- **Bring your history**: Import conversations from other LLMs

## Philosophy

AI amplifies thinking, not just writing. Your vault is your second brain. Apollo makes it intelligent.

Built on ideas from [Claudesidian](https://github.com/heyitsnoah/claudesidian) by Noah Brier.
