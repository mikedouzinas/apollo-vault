# Areas

Ongoing responsibilities without end dates.

## What Goes Here

- Life domains you maintain continuously
- No deadline, no specific completion criteria
- Create dedicated notes with links to related resources

## Examples

- Health & Fitness
- Professional Development
- Finances
- Family
- Career
- Learning

## Difference from Projects

Projects end. Areas don't. "Write a book" is a project. "Writing practice" is an area.
